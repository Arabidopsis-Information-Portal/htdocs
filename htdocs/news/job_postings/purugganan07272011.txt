POSTDOC IN PLANT EVOLUTIONARY/ECOLOGICAL SYSTEMS BIOLOGY



A postdoctoral scientist position to study rice evolutionary/ecological
systems biology is open in the laboratory of Michael Purugganan at the NYU
Center for Genomics and Systems Biology. Applicants should have (1) a
strong background in plant molecular biology/systems biology, (2) experience
in experimental and computational analyses of genome-wide gene expression
and (3) interest in plant evolutionary and ecological genomics. Interested
parties can email an inquiry and their CV to mp132@nyu.edu
