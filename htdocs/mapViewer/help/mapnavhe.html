Navigating Maps

When you select to view maps, either by browsing or searching, the MapViewer window appears. This window has two major sections: the central controls and the maps.

Central Controls

The central controls appear at the top of the window and let you update the selection of maps displayed; scroll and zoom in all visible maps; search maps; and display a printable version of the visible maps. For help with these actions see "Using the Central Controls".

Maps

One or more maps will appear beneath the central controls. On the left side and at the top of each map are map controls you can use to scroll, zoom, search, or move the map view. For help with these actions, see "Using the Individual Map Controls".

The content of each type of map varies. For specific information about a type of map see "Close-Up on Sequence Maps", "Close-Up on Genetic Maps", or "Close-Up on Physical Maps".
