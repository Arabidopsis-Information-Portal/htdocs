Finkelstein

Available for Chromosome II. The Finkelstein map is a physical map in which the sizes of the clones, most of the probes, and the distance between probes are known or can be inferred.

The data for this map for obtained through personal contact with Ruth Finkelstein.

For more information about the physical map display, see "Close-Up on Physical Maps" and "Physical Maps Color Key".
