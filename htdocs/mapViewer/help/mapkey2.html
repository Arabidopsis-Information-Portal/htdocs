AGI

The AGI map displays information about clones chosen for sequencing by the AGI (Arabidopsis Genome Initiative). Types of information shown on this map include clone position, size, sequencing status, overlaps with adjacent clones and the position of markers within the clones. The information used in the construction of the AGI map is gathered from the individual AGI member labs and updated approximately twice a month. The position of contigs on the map is based on the RI map positions of markers that fall within the contig. Contigs that lack RI markers do not appear on the map but can be viewed by selecting "Clones with uncertain locations" to the left of the map. The clones in the resulting table are sorted by nearest RI marker.
