


LABORATORY  TECHNICIAN – MOLECULAR BIOLOGY

Applications are invited for a Laboratory Technician or Research Assistant to provide 
molecular biology research support to the group of Professor Jonathan Jones, 
studying plant-microbe interactions (see www.tsl.ac.uk/jj ).  The post holder will 
provide scientific support for laboratory projects, will organise and provide continuity 
for laboratory resources, and will assist in the day-to-day running of the Jones group. 

The Sainsbury Laboratory is a charitable company of ~80 research scientists and 
support staff and a world leader in plant science.  We are based on the Norwich 
Research Park and closely linked to the Gatsby Charitable Foundation, University of 
East Anglia and the John Innes Centre.

For more information visit www.tsl.ac.uk

Applicants should have at least a degree in biological sciences, or equivalent, and 
should have strong skills in and enthusiasm for molecular biology. Previous 
experience with plants is an advantage, but is not essential. The successful applicant 
will have excellent organisational and interpersonal skills and be highly motivated. 

This fixed-term position is for three years in the first instance and will be available 
from April 2008.  The salary, Grade 6/7 on the UEA scale, will be in the range 
£21,682 - £32,796, the appointment level reflecting qualifications, skills, knowledge 
and achievements.

Applicants should provide a CV, including the names and contact details of two 
referees, and a covering letter addressing the selection criteria and their motivation 
for seeking the position.  Please send formal applications, quoting the reference 
number below, either by e-mail to HR@TSL.ac.uk or by post to Kim Blanchflower, 
HR Officer, The Sainsbury Laboratory, John Innes Centre, Norwich Research Park, 
Colney, Norwich  NR4 7UH, UK.  

Applications will be considered from Friday February 1st, 2008, and the position is 
open till filled.    Ref: JJ01/2008
