GRADUATE RESEARCH FELLOWSHIPS IN BIOINFORMATICS

Ohio University

We are seeking applicants with a strong background in both computer science and

biology to pursue a PhD degree in the field of bioinformatics.

Students will be part of team of engineers and scientists who are working

on genomics and proteomics research with faculty in the Molecular and Cellular 
Biology Program,

the Biomedical Engineering Program, and the Edison Biotechnology Institute.

Qualified applicants should submit their CVs to Dr. Lonnie Welch at 
welch@ohio.edu.
