Figure 4. Summary of the 1.7 Mb clone contig. The positions of the cosmids are illustrated relative to projected SalI or BamHI restriction maps, as indicated. The numbers in boxes represent the sizes of restriction fragments as determined by summation of the sizes (determined by agarose gel electophoresis) of fragments produced by cleavage of cosmids and BACs with restriction endonucleases. The approximate positions of selected YACs from the YAC-based physical map of this region of the genome are idicated by broken, double-headed arrows.
