  • POSTER INSTRUCTIONS

Please submit Abstracts on-line through this website. Abstracts can be submitted for posters or oral presentation consideration. If you submit an abstract for oral consideration and it is NOT selected, it will become a poster abstract submission.

The following information is relevant to the submission process.

  • Abstracts must be written in English.
  • Abstracts must be submitted online. Emailed and faxed abstracts will not be accepted.
  • The name of the presenter must be indicated, and all presenters must register for the conference.
  • Select the category that is most appropriate for your abstract.
  • Indicate whether your abstract is to be considered for a short oral presentation.
  • For consideration in a concurrent session, your Abstract must be submitted by April 30.
  • Those selected for oral presentations are also expected to present a poster.
  • Oral presentation selections will be posted on the conference website by June 2.
  • Only those Abstracts for which have been submitted by May 30 will be included in the printed Abstract book.
  • Submitters will receive email confirmation of a submission and will be issued an abstract ID number.
  • Abstracts are limited to 2000 characters.

Photographing, video taping, or recording of any kind IS PROHIBITED (including but not limited to camera phones and digital devices) The maximum size allocated for each poster is 4' x 4'. The poster boards will hold two posters on each side. Posters may be located in several rooms and will be organized by category.
