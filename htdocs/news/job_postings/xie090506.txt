The Department of Biological Sciences at Texas Tech University is inviting applications 
for a Postdoctoral Research Associate position. The position is available immediately to 
study small RNA and related pathways in plants. The successful candidate will join the 
laboratory of Dr. Zhixin Xie, which is housed in the newly completed Experimental Sciences 
Building equipped with state of the art research facilities. We are looking for highly 
motivated candidates with strong backgrounds in plant molecular biology. Minimum 
requirement for this position includes a PhD degree in Molecular Biology, Biochemistry 
or a related field, excellent communication skills, and experience of working with a plant 
model. Prior experience with RNA analyses or protein expression and protein interaction 
studies are highly desired, though not required. The successful applicant will be offered 
a competitive salary commensurate with experience, and a benefit package. Qualified 
applicants are invited to !
 send a CV and a list of three references to the attention of Dr. Zhixin Xie 
(E-mail: zhixin.xie@ttu.edu), Assistant Professor, Department of Biological Sciences, 
Texas Tech University; P. O. Box 43131; Lubbock, TX 79409-3131. Applications will be 
accepted until a suitable applicant is identified. Texas Tech University is an Equal 
Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer
