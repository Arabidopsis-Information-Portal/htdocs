Postdoctoral position in developmental biology at Boyce Thompson Institute

A three-year postdoctoral position (job #:2007-09) is available in
Ji-Young Lee’s lab at Boyce Thompson Institute for Plant Research,
starting in September, 2007.  The goal of the research is to understand
the development and transcriptional regulatory networks of the procambium
and its relation to the vascular tissue development in Arabidopsis roots.
A job candidate should have a good understanding of plant development with
strong skills in molecular biology and genetics.  Experience with
microarray data analysis or other mathematical analysis will be helpful
but not required.

To apply, go to http://bti.cornell.edu/hr/hr_appl/hr_appl.php. The
application will be reviewed upon receipt and the position will remain
open until filled.
