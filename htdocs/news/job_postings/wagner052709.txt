Postdoctoral Position University of Pennsylvania 
A Postdoctoral Position is available at the University of Pennsylvania in the laboratory of Dr. Doris 
Wagner. The project investigates how the onset of reproduction is regulated in Arabidopsis with 
emphasis on the role of the plant specific transcription factor LEAFY in control of flower formation 
and patterning. The successful applicant will employ system-wide approaches such as ChIP-
chip/seq as well as genetic and molecular analyses. The position requires a Ph.D. in Biology with 
experience in Developmental Biology, Molecular Biology and high-throughput analyses. 
 
To apply, please send a CV, a statement of research experience and research interests, and the 
names of three references (address, phone, and email) to wagnerdo@sas.upenn.edu.
