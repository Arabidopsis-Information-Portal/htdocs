The Arabidopsis thaliana Database Project (AtDB) ended on August 31, 1999. Starting October 1, the AtDB project has been succeeded by The Arabidopsis Information Resource (TAIR), a collaborative effort between the Carnegie Institution of Washington Department of Plant Biology and the National Center for Genome Resources (NCGR).

TAIR web interfaces are currently hosted at NCGR. The format and structure of the interfaces are essentially identical to those at AtDB. AtDB's database will be retired by the fall of 2000. We are planning on releasing the new database and web navigation structure in the fall of 2000. A preliminary version of the database and interfaces will be released in June of 2000. Changes into the new TAIR interfaces will be incremental and with ample notice.

All changes and updates will be posted on both the AtDB and TAIR sites, and will also be announced to the Arabidopsis newsgroup.
Any questions or problems during this transition period should be addressed to the TAIR curators at: curator@arabidopsis.org
