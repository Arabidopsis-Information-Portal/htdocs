ARNOLD, C.G.

An x-ray mutant with eight chromosomes

An X1 (X-rayed as zygotes) among other plants which varied from the normal Arabidopsis thaliana one plant was extremely different. The mutant had smaller leaves and petals, nine extraordinary thin stems; it was nearly sterile and produced only few seeds, even after pollination with normal pollen. Cytological control showed 8 chromosomes. Further studies of the X1-progeny will follow.
