Submit BAC Details to TAIR


THIS FORM IS FOR YOU TO PROVIDE BAC MAPPING-DATA TO TAIR.

In this form, you tell us who you are, the probe you've used and the BACs that probe hits. Please complete a new form for each probe used. If you already have your data in some sort of computer format, which you think may obviate the need to complete this form, please read this first Tips on how to fill in Web forms are available, if you're unsure of how to proceed. If you have more than 10 BACs hit by your probe, please use the extra-space version.

The purpose of this form is to enable TAIR to collect BAC-mapping data. This will be of use to the community in general for mapping and also of specific use for the genome sequencing projects. All BAC, and other physical-mapping, data submitted to TAIR will be displayed soon in TAIR database.

If you have details regarding the TAMU BACs only, and prefer not to complete this Web form, Rod Wing (from the TAMU BAC project and now at Clemson University) is, as announced on the Arabidopsis Bulletin board, collecting such data via e-mail. He will then collate and forward the information to TAIR.
