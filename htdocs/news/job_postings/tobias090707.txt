USDA-ARS Posdoctoral Research Affiliate
Switchgrass marker development and linkage mapping.
 
The following position is available with funding for two years to establish EST-
SSR based markers and collaborate with external groups in development of a 
publicly accessible linkage map of switchgrass that will be integrated with the 
genetic maps of related grass species and accessible through public databases.  
 
Qualifications include:
1. Hands on experience in molecular markers development
2. Experience constructing genetic and physical maps. Experience with
   autopolyploid species will be welcome
3. Ability to work in a team environment
4. Excellent oral and written communication skills
 
Send a cover letter discussing your qualifications along with your CV to
ctobias@pw.usda.gov
