Mary Anderson

Mary is Director of the Nottingham Arabidopsis Stock Centre (NASC). She is also curator of the NASC, UKPGRG and PHYTOS WWW servers and germplasm and DNA stock information for AAtDB (An Arabidopsis thaliana database). She also produces "Weeds World", the International electronic Arabidopsis Newsletter.
