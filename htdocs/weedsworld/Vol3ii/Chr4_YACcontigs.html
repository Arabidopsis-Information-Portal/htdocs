These data relate to the paper: Renate Schmidt, Joanne West, Gerda Cnops, Karina Love, Alma Balestrazzi, and Caroline Dean (1996). Detailed description of four YAC contigs representing 17Mb of chromosome 4 of Arabidopsis thaliana ecotype Columbia. The Plant Journal, 9, 755-765.

Most of these images are very large. If you are having trouble viewing or saving them using your WWW browser, it may be worth transferring the files you want using anonymous ftp from genome-ftp.stanford.edu in the directory /arabidopsis/JIC-contigs.
