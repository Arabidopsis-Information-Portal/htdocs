Explanation of Columns:


  • Locus: Gene symbol (CAPS to avoid confusion between letters and numbers).
  • Descriptive Name: Formal name of locus; description of phenotype.
  • Date: Shows date when symbol was reserved or identified.
    • YB = Already in previous genome report list (Yellow Book, Year 3).
    • M95 = Obtained from abstract book - Arabidopsis meeting, June 1995.
    • N96 = Obtained from WWW abstracts - Arabidopsis meeting, June 1996.
    • M97 = Obtained from abstracts/pages - Arabidopsis meeting, June 1997.
  • Reference Laboratory: Source of mutant gene symbol.
