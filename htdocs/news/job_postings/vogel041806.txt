of alternative, environmentally friendly fuel sources. The successful 
candidate will conduct research aimed at improving the yield and 
digestibility of dedicated energy crops for the production of 
bioethanol. The incumbent would have responsibility for the 
production, care and analysis of transgenic switchgrass plants 
containing transgenes designed to improve switchgrass as an energy 
crop. Excellent oral and written skills are required.  Salary will be 
competitive and commensurate with experience.
