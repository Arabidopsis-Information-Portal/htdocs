AIS Vol 02:

Callus culture of Arabidopsis

J. R. LOWENBERG

(Department of Botany, University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee, Wisc., U.S.A.)

While genetic studies on Arabidopsis are progressing rapidly, physiological and metabolic studies appear to be lagging. One of the reasons for this situation is the size of the organism. In order to obtain an appreciable mass of this material, an investigation to grow Arabidopsis as callus culture has been started.
