AnthraCyc Release Note History

Release Notes for AnthraCyc Version 9.1

Released on May 23, 2005.

24 proteins determined to be in the secretome were assigned a location of extracellular-space. We gratefully acknowledge Dr. Alexander Walz and Dr. Vito DelVecchio of Vital Probes, Inc. for supplying this data, to be published in LaMonica JM, Wagner M, Eschenbrenner M, Williams LE, Miller TL, Patra, G (2005), "Comparative Analyses of Three Bacillus anthracis Strains with Variant Plasmid Contents," Infection and Immunity:73(6).
