Access TAIR's Sequence Clone Registration Form


THIS FORM IS FOR MEMBERS OF AGI TO GAIN ACCESS TO REGISTER CLONES WITH TAIR

This form is solely for members of the international Arabidopsis sequencing effort (AGI -- Arabidopsis Genome Initiative) to register their intention to sequence a given clone. It is password-protected to prevent corruption of the data within TAIR. None of the data submitted is hidden from users of TAIR and is available from TAIR database. Any group intending to sequence hundreds of kilobases of contiguous sequence Arabidopsis genomic DNA may join AGI. Contact David Meinke.
