Arabidopsis in education


Some sites concerned with using Arabidopsis (and other plants too) in education, both at school (up to age 18) as well as at undergraduate and graduate level.

Please send any suggestions for additions to this list to TAIR Curator.

Arabidopsis WWW Molecular Biology and Plant Physiology Laboratory manuals for undergraduates from Jonathan Monroe.
Both manuals cover semester-long projects using Arabidopsis. One to analyze, sequence, and attempt to identify an anonymous expressed gene. The other involves the isolation and analysis of mutants.

PHYTOS Plant Science education network an international network which links plant science and biotechnolgy education programmes at University sites in Australia, the United States and the United Kingdom.

The Charms of Duckweed. Of particular interest to biology teachers and students.
