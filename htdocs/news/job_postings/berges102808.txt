Technician position at the FRENCH PLANT GENOMIC RESOURCE CENTER 
 
 
 
The French Plant Genomic Resource Centre (CNRGV) is a national platform 
responsible for preserving and maintaining plant genomic libraries produced 
in the context of major projects by internationally-renowned laboratories. 
 
 
 
A technician position is available to participate in the achievement of the 
main missions of this laboratory including genomic libraries management and 
screening. See description in the attached file.
