POSTDOCTORAL POSITION IN PLANT CELL BIOLOGY

 

 

 

A postdoctoral position is available to study mechanisms of pollen tube /pistil interactions particularly in relation to guidance. The successful candidate will combine contemporary methods of microscopy with standard molecular biological and biochemical techniques to study the roles of recently discovered proteins involved in pollen tube guidance. Candidates should have a strong background in biochemistry, molecular biology and plant cell biology.

Experience with Arabidopsis and proteomics approaches is desirable but not necessary. We are part of the Center for Plant Cell Biology (CEPCEB) here at UCR that is equipped with state of the art microscopy, proteomics, genomics and bioinformatics facilities with a new capacity for doing chemical genetic screens.
