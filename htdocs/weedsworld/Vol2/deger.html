Arabidopsis thaliana as a Model for Plant Pathogen Interactions

Davis, K.R.: Hammerschmidt, R. (editors) "Arabidopsis thaliana as a Model for Plant Pathogen Interactions". St Paul, USA; American Phytopathological Society (APS) (1993) vii=134pp. ISBN 0-89054-153-1 [En, 170 ref.] The Biotechnology Center, Department of Plant Biology, Ohio State University, Columbus, OH 43210. This multiauthor book is, in part, based on presentations at a symposium entitled "Arabidopsis as a model system for studying plant-pathogen interactions", held at St. Louis, Missouri, USA on August 21st, 1991, in conjunction with the annual meeting of the APS. THe 9 chapters are followed by an extensive bibliography. Published in the Review of Plant Pathology 1994, Vol 73 No. 12"
