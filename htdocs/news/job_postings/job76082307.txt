
Mendel Biotechnology, Inc., invites applications for a Research
Scientist/Research Associate position in plant molecular biology/biochemistry.
The successful applicant will contribute to focused work in advancing
scientific understanding of lead gene products. Expertise in plant molecular
biology, protein biochemistry (western blotting, immunoprecipitation, etc.) and
experience with Arabidopsis are highly desirable. Strong written and oral
communication skills in the English language are considered essential.
Applicants must hold a M.S. or Ph.D. in plant biology, molecular biology, or
related field, and have at least 2 years of demonstrated experience at the lab
bench in the above techniques. Must be a U.S. citizen or have authorization to
work in the U.S.

To apply, please send a letter of interest, CV, and contact information for
three references (including email addresses) to: job76+b@mendelbio.com
