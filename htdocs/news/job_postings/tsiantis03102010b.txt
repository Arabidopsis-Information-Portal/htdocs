2. Part-time Research Technician
Part-time Research Technician (50 per cent FTE) post for up to 38
months funded by the BBSRC, working on the same project. It is
anticipated that the successful applicant will start in the summer of
2010. Previous experience of genetic mapping and molecular biology
techniques is essential and excellent organisational skills and the
ability to multi-task are expected.
Further particulars may be downloaded from the website at
http://www.plants.ox.ac.uk/ and are also available from the
Administrator.
