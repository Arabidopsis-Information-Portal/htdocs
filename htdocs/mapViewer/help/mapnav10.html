Scrolling with the Arrows

Above each map, beside the location indicators, are scrolling arrows:

To scroll left

  • Select the left arrow

The window redisplays with the map illustrating a segment of the chromosome left of and overlapping with the previous segment.

Note: The map viewer window always redisplays with the central controls at the top. You may have to scroll down in your browser window to view the map you want.

Note: Do not use your browser's Back option in the Map Viewer. Doing so may cause unusual results when you use the Zoom option.

To scroll right

  • Select the right arrow

The window redisplays with the map illustrating a segment of the chromosome right of and overlapping with the previous segment.

Note: The map viewer window always redisplays with the central controls at the top. You may have to scroll down in your browser window to view the map you want.

Note: Do not use your browser's Back option in the Map Viewer. Doing so may cause unusual results when you use the Zoom option.
