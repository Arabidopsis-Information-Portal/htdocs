The mutagenic activity of N,N',N' - trimethylnitrosourea (TMMH) and p-tolysulfonyl methylnitrosamide (TSMN)

J. VELEMINSKY and T. GICHNER

(Institute of Exeperimental Botany, Prague, Czechoslovakia)

Two further nitrosoamides have proved to be very strong mutagens for Arabidopsis thaliana. Only the highest frequencies of induced mutations are given in the following table (treatment time 24 hrs, temperature 25*degrees*C). TMNH had no mutagenic activity on Saccharomyces cerevisiae (MARQUARDT et al., 1964); it was, however, strongly carcinogenic on rats (IVANKOVIC et al., 1965). TSMN was not mutagenic on Saccharomyces cerevisiae (MARQUARDT et al., 1964) and not carcinogenic on rats (DRUCKREY et al., 1961).
