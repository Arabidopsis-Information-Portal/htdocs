A PhD Position is available in the lab of Dr. Paloma Mas to study the 
CELLULAR AND MOLECULAR MECHANISMS UNDERLYING CIRCADIAN CLOCK FUNCTION. 
The project involves the use of Genomic, Proteomic, Cellular and Molecular 
approaches to decipher the role of chromatin remodelling on clock 
progression. 

We are seeking highly motivated and outstanding students with a background 
in natural sciences (e.g. Biology, Biochemistry, Functional Genetics, 
Chemistry). 
Funding: EURYI (EuroHORCS-ESF), EMBO YIP and MICINN. 
If interested, please submit your application to paloma.mas@cid.csic.es
