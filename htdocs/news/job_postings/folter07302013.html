A postdoctoral position is available in the Functional Genomics of Plant Development lab of Dr. Stefan de Folter, at LANGEBIO, CINVESTAV-IPN, Irapuato, Mexico.

The focus of the project is the study of transcription factors and hormones involved in gynoecia development in Arabidopsis. The candidate should have experience with Plant Molecular and Cellular Biology techniques, preferable in Arabidopsis, advanced microscopy (Laser dissection microscopy), and bioinformatics (sequence data analyses).

Furthermore, the candidate should have a demonstrated scientific production and good communication skills in English (oral and written). The candidate should have obtained a PhD degree not before 2009, and not received any financial support before for a postdoctoral position from the Mexican National Council of Science and Technology (CONACyT). The position is for one year, and can be renovated for another year.

Candidates should send a letter of intent, curriculum vitae, and names of two references to Dr. Stefan de Folter, sdfolter@langebio.cinvestav.mx. For recent publications of the lab please visit: https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Stefan_De_Folter/contributions/
