JACOBS, M.

On the induction of biochemical mutants

Biochemical mutants have been obtained by means of sterile culture of M2's after mutagenic treatment. The special requirements (amino acids, vitamins, purins and pyrimidines) are being individually established. Mutants resistant to copper have also been found; the test for the genetical basis of resistance shows a single gene dependence. Moreover mutants for resistance to DDT have been selected after spraying or dipping the young plants in a 1:500 solution of the commercial product.
