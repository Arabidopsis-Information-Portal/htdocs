Postdoctoral Position in Plant Molecular Genetics 
King Abdullah University of Science and Technology, Thuwal 

A postdoctoral Research Associate position is available to investigate the 
molecular mechanisms for plant adaptation to environmental stresses such as 
drought, extreme temperatures, and high soil salinity. Applicants with 
demonstrated research experience in molecular biology, biochemistry, 
genomics, proteomics, or metabolomics are welcome. The research will be 
carried out at the Plant Stress Genomics Center, King Abdullah University of 
Science and Technology (KAUST) near Jeddah, Saudi Arabia (www.kaust.edu.sa). 
Please send your application to liming.xiong (at) kaust.edu.sa.
