AGI Preliminary & Released Sequence
The policy regarding sequence release varies between groups. Some release to GenBank once the sequence is complete and annotated, while other groups submit their preliminary sequence to the HTG (high throughput genomic) section in GenBank. Both preliminary and annotated sequence are included in the main dataset that is used by TAIR's Arabidopsis BLAST search form. Other datasets can also be queried from this form, these are listed below. Together, they provide access to all public Arabidopsis sequences. Furthermore, some groups provide BLAST searches on their as yet unreleased (to GenBank) sequence or on BAC end sequences. CSHL also provide a BLAST server to Arabidopsis repeat squences.

Arabidopsis BLAST searches:
  • TAIR BLAST: can search:
    (i) All Arabidopsis GenBank EST & BAC ends, or
    (ii) Recent = Arabidopsis GenBank Sequences New/Changed in the last Month, or
    (iii) NRAT = Non-Redundant Arabidopsis Protein, or
    (iv) BACEND = BAC End Sequences for Arabidopsis, or
    (v) EST = EST Sequences for Arabidopsis, or
    (vi) Annotated = Arabidopsis GenBank Entries minus EST & BAC ends, or
    (vii) CSHLPrel = CSHL/WASHU Preliminary Arabidopsis Genomic Sequences, or
    (viii) Mendel = Mendel Plant Gene Nomenclature Database (Higher Plant Proteins), or
    (ix) PlantDNA = Higher Plant Sequences from GenBank.

  • Wash. U.: For sequencing by the CSH-WU-ABI consortium

  • CSHL's AtRepBase of repeated sequences in Arabidopsis

  • TIGR: Search TIGR's BAC end sequences.

  • MIPS: Search ESSA sequences.

AGI BAC Contig Fingerprints
Washington University's BAC fingerprint database can be searched for likely matches to specified clones. Note that Wash. U. currently uses non-standard names, i.e., they use padded (leading zeros) and I is used for the IG(F) library and M for the T(AMU).

AGI Sequencing Progress and Status
To view the graphic or tabular reports on the progress of the AGI project or the status of a particular region click on the "TAIR's Database" below. Once at the database starting page select the "AGI" from the table of contents. The graphic view is stated by selecting "Map-based search." For a tabular view change the search criteria in the left frame and then press submit. An additional link is provided that goes to a table of AGI Sequencing Totals. Go to TAIR's database.
