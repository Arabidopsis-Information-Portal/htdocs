A postdoctoral /Research Scientist position is available to study cation
transporters in plants (http://www.bcm.edu/cnrc/faculty/?PMID=9542). The
project integrates membrane transport, genetics, physiology, biochemistry
and nutrition. The person working on the project will be trained in animal
and human nutrition. A scientist with experience in membrane transport
biology is strongly desired. Baylor College of Medicine in Houston Texas
offers a unique setting to conduct plant research. Potential applicants
should send a CV and a list of three references to Kendal Hirschi
(kendalh@bcm.tmc.edu).

Baylor College of Medicine is an Equal Access/Equal Opportunity/Affirmative
Action Employer. Women and individuals of underrepresented groups are
strongly encouraged to apply.
