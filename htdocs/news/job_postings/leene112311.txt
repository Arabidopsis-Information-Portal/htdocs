
VIB-PSB recruits 5 new Postdocs 

The successful post-doctoral applicants will be highly motivated scientists, able to work 
both independently and co-operatively as a member of one of the 18 research groups of the 
[1]Department of Plant Systems Biology ([2]www.psb.ugent.be). He/she must have published 
articles in top ranking scientific journals. 

Appointment is initially for two years. [3]http://www.psb.ugent.be/jobs/579-5-postdoctoral-positions 


Interested candidates should send a curriculum vitae, a letter of motivation with indication 
of their preferred research group (see [4]http://www.psb.ugent.be/contacts/group-leaders ) 
and the names of three referees by e-mail to [5]Christine Tiré This e-mail address is being 
protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it . ([6]chtir@psb.ugent.be) 


The call remains open until all five positions are filled. 

The [7]Department of Plant Systems Biology belongs to the [8]VIB (www.vib.be) and 
[9]Ghent University ([10]www.ugent.be) both equal opportunity employers.
