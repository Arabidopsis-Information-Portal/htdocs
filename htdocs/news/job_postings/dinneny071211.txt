POSTDOCTORAL POSITION AVAILABLE 
Carnegie Institution for Science, Department of Plant Biology 
  
A postdoctoral position is now available in the Dinneny lab at the Carnegie Institution 
for Science, Department of Plant Biology. Our lab is interested in the mechanisms 
governing the response of roots to high salinity and moisture variation in the 
environment. Additional information regarding our research interests can be found at 
our website (http://dpb.carnegiescience.edu/labs/dinneny-lab). Candidates for the 
position are encouraged to work with the PI to develop projects aimed at generating a 
spatiotemporal understanding of plant-environment interactions. Candidates with a strong 
background in the use of genomics and systems biology approaches and/or those with a 
keen interest in developmental biology are encouraged to apply. Experience in the use of 
quantitative genetics is also of interest. 
  
Applicants are expected to have a strong publication track record, exhibit a creative 
scientific approach in their work and enjoy collaborative interactions. Interested 
applicants should send their cover letter and CV with contact information for 3 
references to José Dinneny (dinneny@stanford.edu). Please include in the cover letter a 
description of your research interests and career goals. The position will initially be 
available for 1 year with renewal of up to 4 years based on the performance of the 
individual.
