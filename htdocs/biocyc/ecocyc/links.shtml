E. coli Internet Resources

This page summarizes various Internet resources about Escherichia coli. This page was written by Peter D. Karp; please send suggestions for additional sources to add to .
  • CGSC: E. coli Genetic Stock Center -- A database of E. coli strains, genes, and mutations at Yale University.
  • Center to facilitate the study of shiga toxin producing E. coli at the University of Michigan.
  • Colibri and Metalgen from the Pasteur Institute. Last updated 5/1999.
  • ECDC -- A database of the genetic map of E. coli.
  • EcoGene/EcoMap/EcoSeq
  • EcoSal, the online version of Escherichia coli and Salmonella (subscription required)
  • E. coli Cell Envelope Protein Data Collection at Cardiff University
  • E. coli Database Portal contains many links to E. coli databases on the web.
  • E. coli Index -- Includes Echobase, which contains information about genes and proteins characterized since the E. coli genome was sequenced, and other resources for E. coli researchers and teachers.
  • E. coli Statistics
  • Essential genes in E. coli Last updated 3/2004.
  • GenoBase
  • GenProtEC -- E. coli genome and proteome database at Marine Biological Laboratory, Woods Hole
  • Harvard-Lipper Center for Computational Genetics. -- E. coli resources including microarray datasets and predicted location of DNA-binding sites for various proteins.
  • Microbial evolution laboratory at Michigan State University. Includes links to several culture collections.
  • OU MCF -- Oklahoma University Microarray Core Facility
  • PEC "Profiling of the E. coli chromosome." -- A compilation of information from various sources.
  • Project CyberCell
  • RegulonDB
  • Short repeated elements in E. coli and some other enterobacteria.
  • University of Wisconsin E. coli Genome Project
