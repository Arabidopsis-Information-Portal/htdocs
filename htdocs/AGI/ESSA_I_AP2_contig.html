ESSA I Contig

This page provides links to the ESSA I AP2 contig entries in DDBJ, EMBL and GenBank. This represents two 200 kb contiguous regions from the FCA region of chromosome 4, between RI markers m214 and AP2. More details on the AP2 contig may be found at MIPS.

Note that all the cosmid clones listed below are derived from one YAC (CIC7A10) and that this is reflected in the nomenclature used.

Please be aware that this page, and the related one about the ESSA I contig, are special cases. Clones from within this contigs, unlike other AGI entries in AtDB's database, are not, at present, linked in with clone entries in the database. Clones may be ordered from ABRC.
