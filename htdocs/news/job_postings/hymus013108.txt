Research Associate - Plant Physiology 
Mendel Biotechnology, Inc., Hayward, CA, USA,  
 
Mendel Biotechnology, Inc., invites applications for a Research Associate in Plant 
Physiology. The candidate will aid and perform both leaf and whole-plant experiments on 
Arabidopsis. A Bachelors degree in Biology with emphasis on plant physiology and 
interest in photosynthesis, stress physiology or plant water use would be desirable. 
Applicants must be a U.S. citizen or have authorization to work in the U.S. 
 
To apply, please send a letter of interest, CV, and contact information for three references 
(including email addresses) to: job101@mendelbio.com
