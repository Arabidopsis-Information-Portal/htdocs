Chromosome studies in Arabidopsis thaliana

Lottie M. STEINITZ-SEARS and Suzanne LEE CHEN

(Genetics Department, University of Missouri, Columbia, Missouri, U.S.A.

On the basis of occasional good pachytene to dipolotene figures, it was determined that trisomic II involves the main nucleolar chromosome (figures 1 a-d). It was reported earlier (CHEN and SEARS 1967) that II carries REDEI's linkage group 4. The weakest trisomic, which had been lost, was reobtained among the offspring of 3n material. It is shown in figues 1 e-h. The linkage group of this chromosome is not yet known.*
