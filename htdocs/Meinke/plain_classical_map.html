Classical Genetic Map

Classical map of mutant genes of Arabidopsis. Information was current as of July 10, 1998. Data are organized into columns which show the map location in cM and locus name. Genes marked with an asterisk (*) were initially placed on the recombinant inbred map based on recombination data with molecular markers and then transferred to the classical map as described in this link. Loci which are cloned or have a protein product identified are marked with a plus (+). Map locations shown here are estimates based largely on 2-point recombination data. The orders of closely-linked genes may therefore differ slightly from those shown.
