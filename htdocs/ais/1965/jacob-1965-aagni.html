AIS Vol 02:

Free amino acid composition of Arabidopsis seeds

J. JACOBS

(Laboratory for Genetics of Higher Plants, Faculty of Sciences, University of Brussels, Belgium)

Seeds of race Wilna have been analysed for their amino acid composition by using a Beckman-Spinco apparatus. 100 mgs. seeds have been utilised.

The results show that the concentratrations used in culture media are clearly higher than seed content. Extensive analysis of morphological mutants for qualitative and quantitative content of amino acids are now underway.
