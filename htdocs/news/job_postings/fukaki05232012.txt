Department of Biology, Graduate School of Science, Kobe University, Japan 
Senior Assistant Professor or Associate Professor of Plant Molecular and 
Cell biology 

Applications are invited for a faculty position in the Department of 
Biology, Graduate School of Science, Kobe University, Japan. Candidates 
should have expertise and a commitment to future research and education in 
the area of plant molecular and cell biology. 
  Our policy for promoting diversity welcomes foreign and female 
applicants. 

1. Job type: Lecturer or Associate Professor (Full-time) 
      The position is tenured, subject to Kobe University's mandatory 
retirement age of 65. 

2. Number of positions: 1 

3. Qualifications: Candidates must have a PhD and/ or MD degree 

4. Deadline: Application materials must be received by July 31, 2012 

5. Starting date: October 1, 2012 - April 1, 2013 

6. The following documents should be printed in application materials 
 a) Curriculum vitae 
 b) List of publications 
 c) List of grants supported 
 d) Summary of past research and educational experiences (c.a. 600words) 
 e) Description of future research interests and wishes for education (c.a. 
600words) 
 f) Others (Social activities etc.) 
 g) Names and contact information of two referees
