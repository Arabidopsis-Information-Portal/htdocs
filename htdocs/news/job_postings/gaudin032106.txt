Post-Doctoral Position on Arabidopsis nuclear architecture 
INRA, Versailles, France 

A two-year post-doctoral position is immediately available in our group « Chromatin 
dynamic and gene regulation ». Our group belongs to the Laboratoire de Biologie 
Cellulaire, Institut Jean-Pierre Bourgin, INRA, Versailles, France. The IJPB is an 
attractive and dynamic plant research centre (http://www-ijpb.versailles.inra.fr/) 
and the group has developed connections with chromatin community providing a favorable 
research environment. All facilities for the project are available on site.
The project involves studies of chromatin organization and nuclear architecture during 
differentiation process in the plant model Arabidopsis thaliana.
