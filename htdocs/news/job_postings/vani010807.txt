International PhD Program

1. Germany: International PhD Program

The International Max-Planck Research School for Molecular and Cellular
Biology (IMPRS-MCB) of the Max-Planck Institute of Immunobiology and the
Albert-Ludwigs University of Freiburg in Germany invites suitable
candidates to apply for a position in our International PhD program
2007, which is going to start in October 2007 in Freiburg, Germany. We
invite applications from all countries. 

Details:
http://biojobs.blogspot.com/2007/01/germany-international-phd-program.html 


2. Italy: PhD Fellowships

The International Centre for Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology
offers long and short-term PhD fellowships to nationals from its Member
States at its two Components located in Trieste, Italy, and New Delhi,
India, through the following actions: 

      * International PhD Programme:
        Postgraduate studies (3 years) are available with the aim of
        obtaining the Doctor Philosophiae (PhD) degree in molecular
        genetics and life sciences. The degree can be awarded by a
        number of validating entities of national and international
        level such as: Open University (UK); Scuola Normale Superiore,
        Pisa (Italy); Nova Gorica University (Slovenia) or Jawaharlal
        Nehru University, New Delhi (India). 
      * Flexible Fellowships:
        Sandwich PhD fellowships (6-12 months) are open to PhD
        registered students wishing to carry out part of their
        experimental studies at the ICGEB Laboratories in the framework
        of an agreed collaboration. 

Details: http://biojobs.blogspot.com/2007/01/italy-phd-fellowships.html
