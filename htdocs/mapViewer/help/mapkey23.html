RI

The Recombinant Inbred (RI) lines were generated from a cross of Arabidopsis ecotypes Columbia and Landsberg erecta. The RI map was constructed using a "framework" of markers that acts as a consistent backbone for the map. For chromosomes 2, 4, and 5, which have complete or nearly complete physical maps, the framework is the order of the physical maps. All other markers are placed within the framework using recombination scores. For chromosomes 1 and 3, the framework was generated using recombination data for only those markers that were scored in more than 90 lines.

Markers are assigned to the map by linkage analysis. Two-point data are calculated for all markers and the results used to assign markers to chromosomes based on their linkage to the framework markers. Then, the markers were placed within the chromosome based on three-point data.

As you view these maps, keep in mind that the order of markers and the distances between markers are only as accurate as the quality of data used in the map's construction. Not all markers have been placed with the same certainty.
