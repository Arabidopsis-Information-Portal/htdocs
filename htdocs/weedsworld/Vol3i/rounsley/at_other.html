Arabidopsis thaliana Links

Arabidopsis thaliana is the most widely used plant in molecular biology, and has been adopted as the model plant for genome work. Here are some links to useful web sites that relate to Arabidopsis research.

Arabidopsis Genome Related Pages

Arabidopsis cDNA Sequencing Project

  • The EST's for Arabidopsis come from two main sources - A multi-center lab effort in France, and a collaboration between Michigan State University and the University of Minnesota. Over 70% of the entries in dbEST come from the MSU/UM group. The bioinformatics approach they are using is summarized here.

  • The latest total for Arabidopsis EST sequences in dbEST is given here.
