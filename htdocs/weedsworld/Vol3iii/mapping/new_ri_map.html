Lister and Dean RI Map

Clare Lister and Caroline Dean, John Innes Centre, Norwich, UK arabidopsis@bbsrc.ac.uk

RFLP map generated on Landsberg erecta x Columbia recombinant inbred lines.

June 29 ,1995. 462 markers

The maps for Chromosomes 1, 2, and 3 have been generated completely using "Mapmaker". Chromosome 4 is almost entirely based on the physical map data from Renate Schmidt and her co-workers. The map for Chromosome 5 is partly based on the physical map data.

Any enquiries about markers on the map should be directed to: arabidopsis@bbsrc.ac.uk Please send your segregation data to: LISTER@BBSRC.AC.UK

Further information on the Lister and Dean and other RI lines can be found from the Seed List Contents Page.
