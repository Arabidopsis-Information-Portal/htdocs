Plant and Animal Genome VI meeting

The Plant and Animal Genome V meeting will be held between 18-22nd January 1998, at The Town and Country Hotel, San Diego, California, U.S.A.

Abstracts should be submitted via the WWW PAG-VI abstract form
PAG-VI abstracts will be available for browsing and searching on the Internet once they are accepted sometime between November-December 1997.

The abstract submission deadline is 3 November, 1997.
Registration deadline is 14th November 1997.

A preliminary programme is available.
