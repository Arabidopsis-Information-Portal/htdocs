Starting in Fall 2007, two Ph.D./M.S. assistantships are available in
Botany Department, Miami University to participate in the area of Plant
Genomics and Bioinformatics.  The research work for the selected students
will be an integral component of ConiferGDB (www.conifergdb.org). 
Possible research topics include data mining, database knowledge
discovery, automatic information retrieval and text mining, GO, software
and database development and other bioinformatics applications addressing
various biological questions.  Candidates must have strong computational
background with decent knowledge in plant biology.

Please contact with Dr. Chun Liang (liangc@muohio.edu).

Miami University is an Equal Opportunity Employer which encourages
applications from minority group members and women.
