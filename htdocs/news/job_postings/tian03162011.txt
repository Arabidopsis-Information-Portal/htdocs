Post Doc/Research Associate in Bioinformatics  

Zhixi Tian's lab at Institute of Genetics and Developmental Biology, Chinese Academy of Science, 
China is seeking applications for Post Doc/Research Associate in bioinformatics. The successful 
candidate will use integrated approaches of genetics, compare genomics, and whole genome wide 
association mapping to investigate functional genomics in soybean. Preference will be given to 
applicants with research expertise in plant population genetics, systems genetics. 

Responsibilities:
- Design and perform research projects related to soybean compare genomics independently. 
- Assist research leader in training graduate students.
- Participate in projects application. 

Required Skills/Experience:
- Ph.D in Genomics/Bioinformatics/Computational Biology, have the background of compare genomics.
- Ability to work in a team environment, to track and manage complex projects under the direction of the lead scientist.
- Familiar with Linux system and at least one computer programming language (e.g., Perl), familiar with MySQL.
- Self-motivated person with excellent interpersonal and communication skills. 

Salary is commensurate with experience and qualifications. Please email your cover letter, curriculum 
vitae, and names and addresses of three references to Dr. Zhixi Tian (Zhixi.Tian@gmail.com) and Ms. 
Ning Wang (nwang@genetics.ac.cn).
