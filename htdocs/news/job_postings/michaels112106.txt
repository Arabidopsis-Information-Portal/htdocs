

MULTIPLE POSITIONS IN FLOWERING TIME REGULATION.  Two positions are available in the Michaels lab 
(http://www.bio.indiana.edu/~michaelslab/index.html and http://www.bio.indiana.edu/facultyresearch/faculty/Michaels.html) 
to study the molecular basis of flowering-time regulation in Arabidopsis.  One position will be filled 
at the postdoctoral level; the second may be filled at the research associate or postdoctoral level. 
Experience with plants is helpful, but not required. Interested parties should email a cover letter 
outlining interests, a complete CV, and contact information for two individuals willing to supply 
letters of recommendation to michaels@indiana.edu.
