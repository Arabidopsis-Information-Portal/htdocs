Report on the 7th International Arabidopsis Conference, UEA, Norwich, June 23-27 1996

The conference was dedicated to the memory of Professor Harold Woolhouse, the former Director of the the John Innes Centre who died of cancer the week prior to the conference. Harold was instrumental in bringing Arabidopsis research to the John Innes Centre and was highly influential in the establishment of the Arabidopsis research programme within the UK.
