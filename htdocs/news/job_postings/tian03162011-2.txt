Assistant/Associate Professor Position in Plant Genetics  

Zhixi Tian's lab at Institute of Genetics and Developmental Biology, Chinese Academy of Science, 
China is seeking applications for assistant/associate professor research fellow in plant molecular 
genetics. Preference will be given to applicants with research expertise in plant molecular biology, 
molecular genetics, or cell biology. 

Responsibilities:
- Design and perform research projects related to soybean compare genomics independently. 
- Assist research leader in training graduate students and postdoctoral associates. 
- Participate in projects application and management. 

Required Skills/Experience:
- Ph.D in plant molecular biology or plant molecular genetics.
- Ability to work in a team environment, to track and manage complex projects under the direction of 
the lead scientist.
- Have strong background and good training in plant genetics and molecular biology, familiar with 
cell biology and protein assay would be good plus. 
- Self-motivated person with excellent interpersonal and communication skills. 

Salary is commensurate with experience and qualifications. Please email your cover letter, curriculum 
vitae, and names and addresses of three references to Dr. Zhixi Tian (Zhixi.Tian@gmail.com) and Ms. 
Ning Wang (nwang@genetics.ac.cn).
