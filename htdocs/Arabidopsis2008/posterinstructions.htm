POSTER INSTRUCTIONS

Poster Location
All posters will be located in the Exhibit Hall in the Complexe Desjardins & Alfred Rouleau C. Posters will be organized by category, then by poster number.

Poster Setup
Wednesday July 23, 2008 starting at 5 PM - 9 PM
Posters will be organized in rows by poster category. Selected speakers of concurrent sessions will be listed first. Put your poster on the half side of the board below where your final poster number is located in the corresponding session category. The maximum size allocated for each poster is 4' x 4'. Be sure to use a type size and font that will be easily readable. Poster pins will be provided. Each side can hold two 4' x 4' posters. No electrical or multi-media displays. Do not move the boards or your poster number. If you did not submit your abstract for poster online before the closing of the online site, place your poster in an unnumbered space within the category.

Poster Display Times
All posters will be displayed daily according to the schedule. For security reasons the poster area will be closed after 11 PM. Photographing, video taping, or recording of the posters of any kind is PROHIBITED unless a poster author is present and provides specific permission.

Poster Attendance during the Exclusive Poster Session
Please attend your poster during the exclusive poster session times below according to your final poster number. Those with conflicts during the scheduled times may attach notes to their posters listing alternative times when they will attend their posters.

Exclusive Poster Sessions

Thursday, July 24
8:30 PM - 11:00 PM Poster Session Odd-numbered abstracts presenting

Friday, July 25
8:30 PM - 11:00 PM Poster Session Even-numbered abstracts presenting

Saturday, July 26
12:00 PM - 2:00 PM Lunch with Exhibitors/Open Poster Session

Poster Removal
All posters must be removed at 12 pm, Sunday, July 27, 2008. Posters not removed by 1 pm will be discarded.

Posters not listed in the program book are available online.
