JIC YACs to cosmids on Chr. IV


YACs from the EW YAC library were chosen from across the physical map of chromosome 4 of Arabidopsis thaliana. They were used as hybridisation probes on filters containing DNA from representative cosmids of contigs generated in the Goodman laboratory. Cosmids that showed a positive hybridisation signal were requested from the Goodman laboratory and they were used in Southern confirmation experiments. The results of this work are shown below.

These data were generated by Jo West in the laboratory of Caroline Dean, John Innes Centre, Norwich Research Park, Colney Lane, Norwich, NR4 7UJ, UK. email: arabidopsis@bbsrc.ac.uk
Web display was done by TAIR. Please acknowledge West and Dean for any scientific description of these data.
