Research Scientist Position - Plant Genetics and Cell Biology


Mendel Biotechnology, Inc., invites applications for a Research Scientist
position in plant genetics and cell biology.  The candidate must have
demonstrated ability in plant developmental biology and genetics, including
crosses, genotyping, segregation analysis, mapping, and basic molecular
biology.  Previous experience with Arabidopsis and the extensive genetic tools
available for this plant are highly desired.  Applicants should also have solid
skills in cell biology/microscopy, including working with fluorescently-tagged
proteins.  The candidate should be skilled in the management and tracking of
data using ORACLE and/or MS Access databases, as well as writing scientific
publication quality reports.

Applicants should have a PhD in a scientific discipline with significant
post-doctoral experience, and currently have the ability to work legally in the
US.  Strong written and oral communication skills in the English language are
essential.

To apply, please send a letter of interest, CV, and contact information for
three references (including email addresses) to job77+b@mendelbio.com.
