Postdoc position 
John Innes Centre, Norwich, UK 
A postdoc position is available in the newly established 
group of Dr. Isabel Baurle at the John Innes Centre, Norwich, UK 
(Department of Disease and Stress Biology). The successful applicant 
will join a young and dynamic team studying the role of epigenetic 
mechanisms in the long-term adaptation to abiotic stress with a focus on 
Arabidopsis thaliana. 
The successful applicant holds a degree in a relevant area of biology 
and has experience in molecular biological and genetic techniques. We 
are looking for a highly motivated individual with a strong interest in 
basic research questions, the demonstrated ability to independently 
organize their day-to-day work and the readiness to learn new 
techniques. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills are 
essential as the post holder will work closely with the group leader and 
other members of the team. 
Interested individuals should send a motivation letter, a CV including 
details on past research experience, publications, as well as contact 
details of two referees to isabel.baurle@bbsrc.ac.uk. Application 
screening will begin immediately and continue until the post is filled.
