International Max Planck Research School (IMPRS) 
"From Molecules to Organisms" 

The IMPRS "From Molecules to Organisms" is an international PhD program offered jointly 
by the Max Planck Institute for Developmental Biology, the Friedrich Miescher Laboratory 
and the Eberhard Karls University in Tuebingen, Germany. 

Interdisciplinary research topics cover the fields of structural biology; molecular and cellular 
biology; bioinformatics; molecular genetics in plant and animal models; developmental 
biology; evolution, natural variation and ecology.

We offer:
*	Cutting-edge research projects
*	Excellent research facilities
*	Financial support 
*	Supporting curriculum with many optional courses
*	International environment with English as the working language
