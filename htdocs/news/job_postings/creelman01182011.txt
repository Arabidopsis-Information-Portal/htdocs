*Research Associate 2 (Hayward, CA)* 

Mendel Biotechnology, Inc. invites applications for a Masters level 
researcher to join our Metabolic Profiling/Nitrogen Use team in the 
Technology Division located in Hayward, CA. Candidates with a Bachelors 
degree and multiple years of industry experience will also be considered. 
The successful candidate should have basic training in the areas of plant 
physiology/biochemistry with a minimum of 3 years of laboratory experience. 
 Prior experience with plant metabolomics (e.g. gas chromatography, mass 
spectrometry, high-performance liquid chromatography, thin layer 
chromatography) is also desired. A similar knowledge of biochemical tools 
(e.g. enzyme assays, Western blots) is also essential. The individual must 
have the ability to work across a diverse matrix of teams and must have 
excellent oral and written communication skills. 

Must be a U.S. citizen or have authorization to work in the U.S. To apply, 
please send a letter of interest, CV, and contact information for three 
references (including email addresses) to *job176@mendelbio.com*.
