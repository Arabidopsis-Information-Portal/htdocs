Increasing the effectiveness of mutagenic treatments by inhibitors of metabolism

A. J. MULLER

(Institute of Crop Plant Research, Gatersleben, Kr. Aschersleben, Germany)

Simultaneous treatment with NaN3 or KCN has been found to decrease the frequency of mutations induced by nitrosoalkylureas in seeds of Arabidopsis (VELEMINSKY et al., 1965; MULLER, 1965). Further studies showed that with other mutagenic agents these inhibitors of metabolism have the opposite effect: They enhance the mutagenic effectivness.
