ESSA I FCA Contig


ESSA I AP2 Contig

This page provides links to the ESSA I FCA contig entries in DDBJ, EMBL and GenBank and the locations of clones within the contig. This represents ten 200 kb contiguous regions from the FCA region of chromosome 4, between markers COP9 [classical genetic marker, the nearest RI is mi30] and g3845. More details on the FCA contig may be found at MIPS.

In some cases below, there are two or more clones given, separated by a virgule (foward slash /), this is where the borders of the single cosmids could not be resolved as some ESSA sequencers made shotgun libraries from overlapping clones and submitted the conitiguous sequence.

Please be aware that this page, and the related one about the ESSA I AP2 contig, are special cases. Clones from within this contigs, unlike other AGI entries in AtDB's database, are not, at present, linked in with clone entries in the database. Clones may be ordered from ABRC.
