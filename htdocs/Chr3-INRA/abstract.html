Abstract

We have constructed a YAC contig map of Arabidopsis thaliana chromosome 3. From an estimated total size of 25 Mb, about 21 Mb are covered by 148 clones arranged into 9 YAC contigs, which represent most of the low copy regions of the chromosome. YAC clones were anchored with 260 molecular markers, including 112 for which linkage information was previously available. Most of the genetic map is included in the YAC coverage, and more than 60% of the genetic markers from the reference Recombinant Inbred line map are anchored, giving a high level of integration between the genetic and physical maps. The sub-metacentric structure of the chromosome is confirmed by physical data; 3R (top arm of the linkage map) is about 12 Mb, and 3L (bottom arm of the linkage map) is about 9 Mb. This YAC physical map will aid in chromosome walking experiments and provide a framework for large-scale DNA sequencing of chromosome 3.
