These SSLP data were kindly made available by Marie-Theres Hauser and Tomas Kocabek. The data were posted on the Arabidopsis newsgroup on August 14, 1998.

The sequences of the modified primers are:
nga129b-F: cat aat cga atc gga cac gac g
ateat1b-R: ccc ata tat aac aca ttc acc ac
The sequences of the rest of the primers were previoulsly published by Bell and Ecker.
The sizes of the PCR fragments were estimated from 5% native polyacrylamide gels.
