Fixation of cytological material

L. M. STEINITZ-SEARS

(Department of Field Crops, University of Missouri, Columbia, Mo., U.S.A.)

Fixation of Arabidopsis buds for meiosis can be made in the usual 6 : 3 : 2 (methanol, chloroform, propionic acid) with the addition of 1% "Tween 20". This eliminates the need of evacuation. Good meiotic metaphase preparations have been obtained in this manner, but earlier meiotic stages are difficult to stain.
