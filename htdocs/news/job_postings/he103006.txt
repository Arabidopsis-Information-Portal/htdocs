POSTDOCTORAL POSITION

A postdoctoral position is available at the Department of Biology, San Francisco State University, 
in the laboratory of Zheng-Hui He. The position is available in January 2007 to study molecular 
mechanisms of cell signaling mediated by the cell wall associate kinases under an NIH funded 
project. We are looking for highly motivated candidates with strong backgrounds in biochemistry, 
cell biology and genetics. A strong publication record in international journals and experience in 
protein expression, purification and genetic mapping are desirable  .

Please send your resume, list of publications and three reference letters to zhe@sfsu.edu

For additional information on the nature of our research please visit our website: http://userwww.sfsu.edu/~zhe/
