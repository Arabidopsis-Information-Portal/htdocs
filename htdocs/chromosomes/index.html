Arabidopsis Genomic View

Genomic View is an active graphic of the five Arabidopsis chromosomes, with a few clones or markers serving as landmarks. From this, you can get to any selected location on one of the several map displays provided by AtDB.

Use the "Map Type" pop-up menu to select the map you want: Physical, Genetic and Physical, AGI, Meinke Classical, Lister & Dean RI, or mi RFLP-Marker. Then use the mouse to click on any section of a chromosome or enter a genetic locus, a clone or a probe in text field box and press submit button. The "Selection" display shows the chromosomal basepair coordinates or cM of the zoomed region that will be displayed.

This program was written by Dr. Shuai Weng at AtDB.

You need to enable Java in your browser to view the map!
