This incomplete information indicates that the cross compatibility of Arabidopsis polyploids is similar to that of other species, namely the seed set is better if the female is the higher chromosome number parent. It may be noted furthermore that the hexaploids are separated from the diploids with an almost complete sterility barrier while tetraploids and hexaploids can be intercrossed readily. It appears thus that gene transfer from diploids to higher ploids will be easier stepwise.
