Submit SIMPLE Contig MAP Details to TAIR


THIS FORM IS FOR YOU TO PROVIDE SIMPLE PHYSICAL-MAP CONTIG INFORMATION TO ATDB.

In this form, you tell us who you are, the name of your contig, and which probes hit which clones in your contig. Complete this form if your data is probably best represented as a tiling path as shown below. If you have more detailed mapping-data (physical relationships between markers, etc.), please fill in a Submit Complex Mapping Data form instead.

An introduction to TAIR's forms for submitting physical-mapping data is available, as is a longer explanation and help.

If you already have some hybridization data in some sort of computer format, which you think may obviate the need to complete some of this or the other forms, please read this first.
