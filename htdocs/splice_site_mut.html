Arabidopsis Intron Splice-Site Mutations


Given below is a table of Arabidopsis mutants to intron splice site sequences and their splicing behaviour. This was compiled by John W.S. Brown, Scottish Crop Research Institute. Researchers are encouraged to e-mail new material for this table to him. He will then compile updates to the table, which will be made available on this page by TAIR. The original table was published in, Brown, J.W.S. (1996) Arabidopsis intron mutations and pre-mRNA splicing. The Plant Journal 10, 771-780.

Also available, on separate TAIR pages, are tables of Arabidopis Arabidopsis Intron Splice-Site Consensus Sequences and Exceptions.
