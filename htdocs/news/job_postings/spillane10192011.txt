Postdoctoral Researcher Arabidopsis Genetics & Epigenetics
Plant & AgriBiosciences Centre, National Uni of Ireland Galway

Postdoctoral Researcher (Arabidopsis genetics/epigenetics)
 
The Genetics and Biotechnology Lab of Prof Charles Spillane at the Plant and
AgriBiosciences Centre (PABC), National University of Ireland Galway,
Ireland invites applications from talented Arabidopsis (or plant) molecular
genetics/epigenetics researchers for a post-doctoral opportunity. Enquiries
are invited from applicants who have recently completed their PhD (within
past three years) and have an excellent publication record in high impact
journals. Applicants with interests in genetics/epigenetics of topics such
as plant reproduction, genomic imprinting, polyploidy, seed development
and/or abiotic stress biology are particularly encouraged (including
applicants with significant bioinformatics and genome evolutionary analysis
skills). Prospective applicants should send an e-mail briefly outlining your
research interest and motivations, including; (a) your C.V. (please list
publications and experimental skills) & (b) contact details and e-mail
addresses for 3 referees to: Prof. Charles Spillane, Genetics &
Biotechnology Lab, Plant and AgriBiosciences Centre, National University of
Ireland Galway, Ireland. E-mail: charles.spillane@nuigalway.ie

PABC Website: www.plantagbiosciences.org

Deadline: 18 November 2011
