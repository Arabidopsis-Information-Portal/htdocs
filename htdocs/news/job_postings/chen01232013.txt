We seek highly motivated scientists to test gene expression, epigenetic and 
epigenomic changes in hybrids and allopolyploids of Arabidopsis and cotton. 
Successful candidates should have a Ph.D. in Genetics or Computational 
Biology with good training in molecular biology and epigenetics. 
Bioinformatic and statistical skills in analyzing high-throughput 
sequencing data including RNA-seq, small RNA-seq, methylome-seq, ChIP-seq, 
and genome-seq are helpful. The positions are available immediately. Salary 
is competitive with experience.
