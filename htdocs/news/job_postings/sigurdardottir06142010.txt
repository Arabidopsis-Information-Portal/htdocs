The International PhD Program in the Biological Sciences provides outstanding interdisciplinary 
training in cutting-edge biological research guided by top level scientists in the fields of 
 
- Bioinformatics 
- Structural Biology 
- Plant & Animal Development 
- Molecular & Cellular Biology/ Biochemistry 
- Developmental  & Quantitative Genetics 
- Evolutionary Biology  & Ecological Genetics 

For 3-4 years PhD students receive personal training complemented by selected courses and 
seminars. Degrees are awarded through the University of Tuebingen. All students are fully 
funded. 
 
Highly qualified students receiving their MSc or Diplom degrees by March 31, 2011 are invited 
to apply for Fall 2010 selection. 
 
For further details and access to the online application system please visit
