REVIEW ON THE LINKAGE GROUPS OF ARABIDOPSIS THALIANA (L.) HEYNH.

A.R. Kranz and U. Scheidemann

Fachbereich Biologie (Botanik) J.W. Goethe-Universitat D-6000 Frankfurt am Main, 1 Bundesrepublik Deutschland

The purpose of this short contribution is to summarize all the data on linkage groups in A. thaliana so far published and to encourage further studies in this fundamental field of Arabidopsis research. New and critical comments on marker genes and their localization are necessary (cf. Feenstra, 1978; Koornneef and Van der Veen, 1978; this volume 15 of AIS). Everybody is invited to cooperate with the AIS-secretariate in establishing a standard collection of marker lines.

In the following short description of the loci and their distances (Morgan's map units) so far tested by the authors cited are presented.
