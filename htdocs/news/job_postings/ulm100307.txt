The Spemann Graduate School of Biology and Medicine (SGBM -
www.sgbm.uni-freiburg.de), an interdisciplinary international graduate
school funded by the Excellence Initiative of the German Research Foundation
is recruiting PhD students in the following research fields:

- Molecular Plant Sciences

- Protein Structure and Function

- Neurosciences

- Immunology

- Molecular Medicine

- Developmental Biology

For information about the programme, the research groups and the online
application procedure, please visit the SGBM webpage at:
http://www.sgbm.uni-freiburg.de

Deadline for complete applications: October 15th, 2007. Interviews will take
place early in December. Decisions will be communicated to applicants by end
of December and selected applicants will be able to start as early as
February 2008.
