Scroll Left/Right

You can scroll the view of all maps in the window using the arrows:

To scroll left

  • Select the left arrow

The window redisplays with all maps illustrating a segment of the chromosome left of and overlapping with the previous segment.

To scroll right

  • Select the right arrow

The window redisplays with all maps illustrating a segment of the chromosome right of and overlapping with the previous segment.

Note: Do not use your browser's Back option in the Map Viewer. Doing so may cause unusual results when you use the Zoom option.
