A Postdoctoral Position is available at the University of Pennsylvania in the laboratory of Dr. 
Kimberly Gallagher. The project focuses on the mechanisms of intercellular protein transport in 
the Arabidopsis root specifically looking at the direction and kinetics of transcription factor 
movement between cells. The position requires a Ph.D. in Biology with experience in cell biology, 
molecular biology and confocal microscopy. 
 
To apply, please send a CV, a statement of research experience and research interests, and the 
names of three references (address, phone, and email) to gallagkl@sas.upenn.edu.
