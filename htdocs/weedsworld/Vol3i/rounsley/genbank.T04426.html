            Lambda PRL2 is a cDNA library derived from equal quantities of 4
            pools of mRNA.  The mRNA sources were 1) 7 day germinated etiolated
            seedlings; 2) tissue culture grown roots; 3) staged plants half
            with 24 hour light cycle, half on 16 hr light, 8 hour dark-
            rosettes;  4) same plants as 3 but aerial tissue (stems, flowers
            and siliques.  The vector is BRL's lambda Zip-Lox.  The cDNA
            inserts were directionally cloned with Sal-Not arms using oligo dT
            primed cDNA.  .
