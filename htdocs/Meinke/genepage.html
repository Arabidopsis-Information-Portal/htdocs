Mirror of Mutant Genes of Arabidopsis

Large numbers of mutants have been isolated and characterized in Arabidopsis. This page provides links to detailed information on rules of nomenclature, mutant gene symbols, linkage data, and genetic maps. Additions or corrections should be directed to David Meinke, curator. Technical questions about the presentation of information should be directed to Todd Nickle.

Nomenclature Rules. This explains the rules to be followed when choosing names for genes identified by mutation.

  • Summary of nomenclature rules.

Mutant Gene Symbols. This is a comprehensive list of approved symbols (2 or 3 letters each) for mutant genes. Symbols for genes and other sequences not associated with a mutation are excluded. Refer to this list before choosing a formal name for a new mutant isolated in your lab. Then contact David Meinke to register your mutant name and symbol.

Linkage Information. This is a comprehensive list of mutant genes for which linkage data are available. Follow this link for an explanation of column headings. Synonyms for mutant genes known by more than one name are included.
