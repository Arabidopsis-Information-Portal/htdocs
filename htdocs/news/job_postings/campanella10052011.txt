
POSITION ANNOUNCEMENT

ASSISTANT PROFESSOR, INTEGRATIVE MOLECULAR BIOLOGIST

The Biology and Molecular Biology Department at Montclair State University
seeks applications for a pending position in Integrative Molecular Biology
at the Assistant Professor level to begin September 01, 2012. Position
responsibilities include teaching both physiology/anatomy and molecular
biology at the undergraduate and graduate levels, and establishing a
vigorous, extramurally funded research program involving undergraduate and
graduate students. The candidate will have expertise in using molecular
techniques to investigate integrative, whole organism questions of molecular
biology. A Ph.D. in a relevant field is required prior to September 1, 2012.
Experience in grant seeking/writing and willingness to provide service to
the department, university and larger professional community. Success in
acquisition of grants is desirable. Please submit cover letter and resume
to Dr. James Campanella, Search Committee Chair, Molecular Biologist Search,
Department of Biology and Molecular Biology, Montclair State University,
Montclair, NJ 07043 or electronically to james.campanella@montclair.edu**.
 Screening begins immediately and will continue until the position is
filled.
