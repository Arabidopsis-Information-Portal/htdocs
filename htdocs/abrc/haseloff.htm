GAL4-GFP Enhancer-trap lines
from Dr. Jim Haseloff

 

These GAL4-GFP Enhancer-trap Arabidopsis lines were donated by Dr. Jim Haseloff, MRC Laboratory of Molecular Biology, Cambridge, UK. This lab has regenerated and screened over 8000 T-DNA transformed Arabidopsis plantlets for enhancer-trap driven GAL4 + UAS:mGFP5-ER expression. Several hundred of these GAL4-GFP lines show distinct and stable expression, and these are available from ABRC. For detailed descriptions of these lines, and information relating to these lines, please refer to Jim Haseloff's web page .

 

Line descriptions:
Jxxxx = pre-screened for root expression
Qxxxx = pre-screened for root expression
KSxxx = pre-screened for shoot and floral expression
Mxxxx = prescreened for shoot and floral expression

Sets available:
CS9089, 116 GFP lines (J)
CS9340, 29 GFP lines (Q)
