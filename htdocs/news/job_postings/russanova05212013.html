VIB International Postdoc Program

Dear colleague, dear postdoc,
On 15 th May, we have launched the final call for Omics@VIB applications, for which we have 5 open postdoc positions. Omics@VIB offers international fellowships to junior integrative biology postdocs who have advanced skills in omics technologies. Since the start in 2010, the Omics@VIB program has continuously received increased visibility with 15 fellowships currently appointed, and in this final call we would like to plug into your scientific network in order to further expand the visibility of the program.
What do we have to offer to successful postdocs?
·         a highly stimulating and multicultural environment
·         embedded in excellent research groups
·         involved in breakthrough science
·         access to cutting-edge technologies
·         a 3-year secured salary
·         personal career assistance
How to apply for Omics@VIB fellowships?
·         37 groupleaders are suggesting a topic of their interest on www.vib.be/postdoc webpages : choose your topic and contact the group leader to discuss your project ideas.
·         draft  own integrative biology project, focusing on the use or the introduction of 'omics' ( bioinformatics, metagenomics, genomics, proteomics, ...) and data modelling in the research topic of your choice
·         submit full application with the signature of the host lab (group leader) by 15 th September 2013
Thank you in advance for spreading our message to the most promising postdocs in your network by forwarding this e-mail or printing and distributing the electronic leaflets (download them via www.vib.be/postdoc ) . If you would like to obtain more information, please do not hesitate to contact Lieve Ongena by phone or e-mail if you would have additional questions.
