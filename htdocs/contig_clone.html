THIS FORM IS FOR YOU TO GIVE TAIR DETAILS OF A SPECIFIC CLONE FROM YOUR CONTIG

In this form, you provide us with further details on a clonee from a physical-map contig. You will have previously supplied information on this contig to TAIR using either a Submit Simple- or Submit Complex- Contig Map Details Form. If you have probe, not clone, details, go to the Submit Contig PROBE form instead.

If you haven't filled in a "Submit Contig CLONE Details to TAIR" form before, read about this form before starting. Also available are an introduction to TAIR's forms for submitting physical-mapping data and a longer explanation and help.
