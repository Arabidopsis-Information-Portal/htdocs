mi RFLP-marker Map Table


The table given below shows the mi RFLP-marker map. In each case, the marker map-position, the restriction enzymes used, the insert-size, and fragment sizes in both Columbia and Landsberg erecta are given. Also given is the ABRC Stock Number. Clicking on this will take you to ABRC's AIMS ordering system for that stock.

Also available is an associated mi RFLP hybridization table.

The plasmid used for the mi clones was pUC119. See, Y.G. Liu., N. Mitsukawa, C. Lister, C. Dean, & R.F. Whittier (1996) Isolation and mapping of a new set of 129 RFLP markers in Arabidopsis thaliana using recombinant inbred lines. The Plant Journal, 10, 733-736.

The raw-data file in tab-delimited format, which should be importable into most spreadsheet programs on most platforms, is available.

These data were generously made available by Bob Whittier, Mitsui Plant Biotechnology Research Institute, Japan. ABRC stock numbers were kindly supplied by Doreen Ware of ABRC. Web preparation was by TAIR.
