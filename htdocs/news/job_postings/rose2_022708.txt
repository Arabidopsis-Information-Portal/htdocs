Postdoctoral Research Associate Position.
Postdoctoral research associate position available in the Department of Plant Biology, 
Cornell University, to work on the development and characterization of new sustainable 
biofuel crops and the application of plant cell wall modifying enzymes for 
lignocellulosic conversion. Applicants should ideally have a background in plant cell 
wall biology, enzyme and polysaccharide biochemistry, and experience with diverse 
molecular analytical techniques. Strong written and oral communication skills are 
highly desirable. Please send a curriculum vitae and the names and contact information 
of three referees to Dr. Jocelyn Rose (jr286@cornell.edu). 
http://labs.plantbio.cornell.edu/rose/
