Studies on growth and development of several races of Arabidopsis thaliana

Hypocotyl growth rate - mean values and variation. Material: Selfed progeny of single plants coming from seed lots received from LANGRIDGE, Canberra. Races: Catania (Sicily); Tsu (Japan); Graz (Austria); Langen (Germany); Martuba (North Africa); Stockholm (Sweden). Procedure: Post-dormancy seeds were wetted, placed in petri plates, cold-treated at -5*degrees*C for three days, and placed on 2% sterile nutrient agar at 18*degrees*C in the dark for the duration of the experiments, except for periodic removal for hypocotyl measurements.
