Junior Specialist Position

 A Junior Specialist position is available at the University of California,
Davis in the Dept. of Plant Biology under the supervision of Dr. Anne Britt
and in collaboration with Dr. Simon Chan working in the general area of
chromosome biology in the model plant Arabidopsis thaliana. The candidate
should be familiar with cloning and in vitro mutagenesis techniques.
 Experience with cytogenetics (chromosome visualization) and/or Western
blotting is also desirable. A bachelor's degree is required.

Applications should be submitted in electronicly, and should include a CV
and two letters of reference. Inquiries and applications should include the
subject line "Junior Research Position" and should be sent to:

abbritt@ucdavis.edu
