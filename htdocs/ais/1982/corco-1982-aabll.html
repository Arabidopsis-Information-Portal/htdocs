FURTHER MUTATION STUDIES WITH DIETHYL SULFATE (DES)

A. Corcos

Department of Natural Sciences, Michigan State University, East Lansing, Michigan 48824, USA

We reported (Corcos, 1981) that the mutagenic agent diethyl sulfate (DES) at the dose of 0.5 ml/100 cc had not affected the germination of M 1 Arabidopsis plants, but had seriously affected the germination of M 2 plants. In view of these results, we decreased the concentration of DES to 0.15 ml/100 cc.

The results of lowering the concentration are given in Table 1.

Table 1: Germination results of M 2 plants

The average germination percentage among the M 2 plants, progeny of plants that were treated with DES at the dose of 0.15 ml/100 cc was 32%. The average germination percentage among the M 2 plants, progeny of plants that were reated with 0.5 ml/100 cc, was 44% (Corcos, 1981). Therefore, lowering the concentration of DES to 0.15 ml/100 cc did not improve the germination of M 2 Arabidopsis plants.
