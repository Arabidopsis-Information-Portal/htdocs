PostDocs in Maize Genetics

Two post-doctoral positions in maize genetics will be available soon at NC 
State University , Raleigh , NC, USA. Both will be funded under an NSF 
grant "Mutant-assisted exploration of natural variation underlying R 
gene-mediated immunity in maize". One position will be suitable for a 
molecular biologist and the other for a quantitative geneticist. 

For more details please contact Dr. Peter Balint-Kurti at 
peter_balintkurti@ncsu.edu
