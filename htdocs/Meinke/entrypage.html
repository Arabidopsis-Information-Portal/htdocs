Mirror of Arabidopsis Genetics / Meinke Laboratory

This mirror site at Stanford is provided in case of service interruption to the main Meinke Laboratory pages (http://mutant.lse.okstate.edu/). Please use this mirror site only if you are unable to access the primary site. The information here is updated periodically but is not necessarily current.

To access information, click on the appropriate section:

Mutant Genes of Arabidopsis: This provides information on nomenclature, mutant gene symbols, and linkage data.

Embryo-Defective Mutants: This provides information on embryo-defective mutants isolated and characterized in the Meinke laboratory.
