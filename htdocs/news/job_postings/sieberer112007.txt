Postdoctoral Position in Plant Molecular Biology 
at the Max F. Perutz Laboratories of the Vienna Biocenter 
in Vienna, Austria
 
 
A 2 ½  year postdoctoral position is available in the lab of Tobias Sieberer 
(Max F. Perutz Laboratories, Vienna Biocenter, Vienna, Austria, 
http://www.mfpl.ac.at/index.php?cid=595) 
to study the control of stem cell pool size in plant shoot meristems by functional 
characterization of the AMP1 pathway.
 
We are looking for a candidate with a Ph.D. in Plant Genetics or Plant Molecular Biology 
who is experienced in performing 
genetic screens in Arabidopsis. Experience in molecular cloning, plant tissue sectioning 
and in situ hybridisation is desired. 
If you are motivated, well organized and happy to supervise graduate students please 
apply by sending a letter of application,
 your CV and the names and contact information of three references to Dr. Tobias 
Sieberer, Department for Plant Molecular Biology, 
Max F. Perutz Laboratories, Dr. Bohr-Gasse 9/4, A-1030 Vienna, Austria or by email to 
tobias.sieberer@univie.ac.at
 
We offer a supportive working environment in a young and motivated team at the Vienna 
Biocenter, Austria’s leading Research cluster 
in Life Sciences. We can provide a comprehensive salary combined with a high living 
standard in the pulsating center of Europe.
 
The position is available at the beginning of 2008. Closing date for applications: 20. 
Dezember 2007.
