SUMMARY

The paper gives a preliminary description of the mutant form 90 of A. thaliana marked by fasciation of the main stem. The fasciation effect was investigated during cross-breeding of the mutant 90 (mace-looking pods) with the lu'co genotype. It is assumed that the selected form is a triple recessive of the fifth linkage group.
